Here's a classic cheesecake recipe for you:

**New York Style Cheesecake**

**Ingredients:**

*Crust:*
- 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
- 1/4 cup melted butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar

*Filling:*
- 4 packages (32 oz) cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup sour cream

**Instructions:**

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press into a 9-inch springform pan
3. Beat cream cheese until smooth, add sugar and vanilla
4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each
5. Fold in sour cream
6. Pour filling over crust
7. Bake for 45-55 minutes until edges are set but center slightly jiggles
8. Turn off oven, crack door open, let cool for 1 hour
9. Refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving

**Tips:**
- Use room temperature ingredients for smooth batter
- Don't overmix to prevent cracks
- Run a knife around edges before removing springform

Enjoy! Let me know if you'd like any variations or have questions.
