Here's a simple pancake recipe for you:

**Basic Pancakes**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (plus extra for cooking)

**Instructions:**
1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl
2. In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, and melted butter
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (lumps are okay)
4. Heat a pan over medium heat, add a bit of butter
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake
6. Cook until bubbles form on surface, then flip
7. Cook other side until golden brown

**For tea:** Boil water, add tea leaves/bag, steep for 3-5 minutes, add milk and sugar to taste.
