Here's a simple pancake recipe:

**Basic Pancakes**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

**Instructions:**
1. Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt) in a bowl
2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter
3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix until just combined (lumps are okay)
4. Heat a pan over medium heat, lightly grease with butter
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake
6. Flip when bubbles form on surface (about 2-3 minutes)
7. Cook other side for 1-2 minutes until golden

Serve with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings!

For tea: Boil water, add tea leaves/tea bag, steep for 3-5 minutes, add sugar/milk to taste.
