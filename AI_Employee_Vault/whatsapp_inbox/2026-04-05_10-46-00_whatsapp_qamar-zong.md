*New York Style Cheesecake*

*Ingredients:*

Crust:
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup melted butter
2 tablespoons sugar

Filling:
4 packages (32 oz) cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream

*Instructions:*

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press into a 9-inch springform pan
Beat cream cheese until smooth, add sugar and vanilla
Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each
Fold in sour cream
Pour filling over crust
Bake for 45-55 minutes until edges are set but center slightly jiggles
Turn off oven, crack door open, let cool for 1 hour
Refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving
